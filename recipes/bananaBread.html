Healthy Banana bread

Description

Banana bread is simple, comforting treat that's perfect for breakfast or as a snack. This banana bread is 100% healthy and is free of refined sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup mashed banana (320g)
  • 2 tbsp ground flax mixed with 5 tbspwarm water
  • 1/3 cup apple sauce
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 3/4 cup oat flour
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

  • Additional Add ins

  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped dates

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. add ground flax to warm water and let it sti for 10 - 15 minutes
  3. Mix together your wet ingredients, then mix in the dry ingredients
  4. Add in any add ins you'd like
  5. Bake it for 1 hour 15 minutes until the bread has darkened and had a nice crust on the outside
